Pricing Questions & Answers

Are there any setup fees or monthly costs?

No setup fees, no monthly costs, no hidden charges. You pay once for the hardware and own it forever. No ongoing subscriptions required.

What's the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order is 100 plates ($500). Most churches find they need 1-3 plates per pew or seating row for optimal coverage.

How long does shipping take?

Orders typically ship within 2-3 business days and arrive within 3-5 business days via free ground shipping. Rush shipping available upon request.

Can I customize the plates with our church logo?

Yes! All orders include custom branding with your church name and logo. We'll send you a design proof for approval before production.

What if I need to order more plates later?

Additional plates can be ordered anytime at the same pricing tier as your original order. We keep your branding design on file for easy reordering.

Do you offer any guarantees?

Yes! We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, return the unused plates for a full refund.
