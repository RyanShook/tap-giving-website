Pricing Questions & Answers

Are there any setup fees or monthly costs?

No setup fees, no monthly costs, no hidden charges. You pay once for the hardware and own it forever. No ongoing subscriptions required.

What's the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order is 100 plates. Most churches find they need 1-3 plates per pew or seating row for optimal coverage.

How long does shipping take?

Orders typically ship within 2-3 business days and arrive within 3-5 business days via free ground shipping. Rush shipping available upon request.

Can I customize the plates with our church logo?

Yes! All orders include custom branding with your church name and logo. We'll send you a design proof for approval before production.

What if I need to order more plates later?

Additional plates can be ordered anytime at the same pricing tier as your original order. We keep your branding design on file for easy reordering.

Do you offer any guarantees?

Yes! We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, return the unused plates for a full refund.

How do people know to tap their phone on the plate?

Each plate comes with clear instructions and a QR code backup. Most people are familiar with NFC technology from contactless payments. We also provide instructional materials for your congregation.

What if someone doesn't have NFC on their phone?

Every plate includes a QR code that works with any smartphone camera. This ensures 100% compatibility across all devices, regardless of NFC capability.

How long does it take to set up?

Setup takes just 5-10 minutes. We provide step-by-step instructions and configure everything to work with your existing giving platform before shipping.

Do the plates need to be charged or plugged in?

No! The plates are completely passive NFC devices that require no power, charging, or maintenance. Simply place them in your pews and they're ready to go.

Can we track which plates are being used most?

Yes, each plate has a unique identifier that allows you to see usage analytics through your giving platform, helping you understand congregation engagement patterns.

What happens if a plate gets damaged or lost?

Replacement plates can be ordered individually for $5 each. We keep your configuration on file, so replacements work immediately without any setup.

Do you offer training for our staff?

Yes! We provide comprehensive setup guides, video tutorials, and phone support to ensure your team is comfortable with the technology and can help congregation members.

Can the plates work with multiple giving platforms?

Each plate is configured for one giving platform at a time. If you change platforms, we can reconfigure your plates for a small service fee.

Are there any ongoing costs or subscriptions?

No ongoing costs with Tap.Giving! You only pay the one-time hardware cost. Any processing fees come from your chosen giving platform (Tithely, Subsplash, etc.).
