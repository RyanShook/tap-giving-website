Common Questions from Tithe.ly Churches

“Do I need to switch away from Tithe.ly?”

Not at all. Tap.Giving plates simply open your existing Tithe.ly giving URL. All donations still process through Tithe.ly. Your workflow doesn’t change at all.

“Tithe.ly is already free. Why would I pay for plates?”

Tithe.ly’s free plan is great for online giving. But online only works if people find your giving page. Tap plates put your Tithe.ly link at every seat, every exit, every event—right where generosity happens. A single $3.50 plate pays for itself with one tap.

“Will my giving data still show up in Tithe.ly?”

Yes. Since donors go directly to your Tithe.ly giving page, every donation shows up in your Tithe.ly dashboard, donor profiles, and year-end reports. Tap.Giving doesn’t touch your data.

“Do visitors need the Tithe.ly app?”

No. Tap plates open your Tithe.ly web giving page in the visitor’s browser. No app download, no account creation. Works on any iPhone or Android phone made in the last 10 years.

“Can I use different Tithe.ly giving forms for different plates?”

Absolutely. You can program different plates to link to different Tithe.ly giving forms—one for general giving, one for building fund, one for missions. Each plate can have its own URL. If you ever need to change it, you can reprogram a plate yourself in 30 seconds with a free NFC app.
