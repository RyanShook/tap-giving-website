1. Subsplash Tap: All-in-One Ecosystem

Subsplash Tap is part of Subsplash's comprehensive church platform, offering NFC giving alongside apps, websites, and church management tools. While powerful, it requires full platform adoption.

✅ Pros

  • • Integrated church management
  • • Single vendor relationship
  • • Professional design quality
  • • Comprehensive digital solution

❌ Cons

  • • Must adopt entire platform
  • • High monthly costs ($250+)
  • • Platform lock-in
  • • No pricing transparency

2. Tithely Tap-to-Give: Established Player

Tithely is a well-known giving platform that added NFC capabilities. Popular among churches but comes with significant setup and ongoing costs.

✅ Pros

  • • Established brand reputation
  • • Good customer support
  • • Professional hardware design
  • • Integrated giving platform

❌ Cons

  • • $1,200 setup fee
  • • $49-199/month ongoing costs
  • • $25/plate hardware cost
  • • Platform lock-in

3. Tap Tag: Budget Basic Option

Simple NFC tags that redirect to giving pages. While affordable, they lack the professional appearance and functionality of dedicated solutions.

✅ Pros

  • • Very low cost ($2-5/tag)
  • • No monthly fees
  • • Platform flexibility
  • • Simple implementation

❌ Cons

  • • Unprofessional appearance
  • • Limited customization
  • • May not inspire confidence
  • • Basic functionality

Frequently Asked Questions

Which platform increases giving the most?

All NFC solutions can increase giving by 300%+. The key is ease of use and donor confidence, which professional solutions like Tap.Giving provide while keeping costs low.

Can I switch platforms later?

With Tap.Giving, yes - you own the hardware and can change your giving platform anytime. Platform-specific solutions like Subsplash and Tithely lock you in.

What about security and compliance?

All reputable NFC solutions redirect to secure giving platforms. The security depends on your chosen giving platform, not the NFC hardware itself.

How long does setup take?

With Tap.Giving, setup takes minutes - just place the plates and they redirect to your existing giving page. Platform switches with Subsplash or Tithely can take weeks or months.

What if I'm already using Tithely or another platform?

Perfect! Tap.Giving works with any giving platform including Tithely, Pushpay, Givelify, and more. You can add NFC giving without changing platforms or paying monthly fees.
