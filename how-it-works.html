Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about implementing Tap.Giving

What phones work with Tap.Giving?

Tap.Giving works with 95% of modern smartphones including iPhone 6 and newer, and most Android phones from 2014 onward. The phone must have NFC (Near Field Communication) enabled, which is standard on virtually all smartphones today. No app download required.

How much do the NFC plates cost?

Our transparent pricing ranges from $3.50 to $5.00 per plate based on quantity:

  • • 1000+ plates: $3.50 each
  • • 400-999 plates: $4.00 each
  • • 200-399 plates: $4.50 each
  • • 100-199 plates: $5.00 each

No setup fees, no monthly costs, no hidden charges. One-time purchase that lasts for years.

Where should we place the NFC plates?

Popular placement options include:

  • • Chair/pew backs (most popular)
  • • Bulletins or programs
  • • Entry/exit areas
  • • Connection tables
  • • Lobby displays
  • • Children's ministry check-in

Most churches start with every other row or strategic high-traffic areas, then expand based on usage patterns.

How secure are the donations?

Security is our top priority. All donations are processed through PCI DSS compliant payment processors with bank-level encryption. The NFC plates contain no sensitive information - they simply redirect to a secure donation page. All financial data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we never store payment information.

Do the plates require batteries or maintenance?

No batteries required! NFC plates are passive devices that are powered by the smartphone's NFC field when tapped. They're designed to last for years with zero maintenance. The plates are also waterproof and scratch-resistant, making them perfect for high-traffic church environments.

How long does shipping take?

Typical production and shipping time is 2-3 weeks for standard orders. Rush orders (1 week) are available for an additional fee. We ship throughout the United States via FedEx or UPS with tracking provided. International shipping is available upon request.

Can we customize the plates with our church logo?

Yes! We offer full customization including your church logo, colors, and messaging. Our design team will work with you to create plates that match your church's branding and aesthetic. Custom artwork is included at no additional cost for orders over 200 plates.

What payment methods do donors use?

Donors can give using Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, or any major credit/debit card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover). The checkout process is optimized for mobile and typically takes 15-30 seconds to complete. Recurring giving options are also available.
