How Much Does Tithely Cost? (Free & Paid Tiers)

Tithely uses a freemium model. You can accept online donations with no monthly subscription on the free plan, and you only pay for software when you want the broader suite of tools like church management, sites, events, and messaging.

Here is the public pricing structure as listed on Tithely's own site. Confirm with tithe.ly/pricing before budgeting, because plan names and exact numbers change.

Tithely Plan What's Included Monthly Cost
Tithely Giving (Free) Online giving, text giving, recurring gifts, basic donor records $0
Tithely ChMS (standalone) Church management, people, groups, check-in ~$99/mo
Tithely Elevate Bundled ChMS + Giving + Messaging + Events ~$119/mo
Tithely Sites Church website builder and hosting ~$50-$100/mo
Tithely Tap (hardware) NFC tags/plates encoded to a Tithely giving page ~$5-$10/tag

Figures reflect Tithely's publicly listed pricing as of April 2026 and may change. For larger multi-campus churches, Elevate tiers can reach $149/month or higher. Annual billing typically unlocks a discount.

Tithely Giving Fees: Transaction Costs Explained

Even on the free plan, every online donation carries processing fees. These are paid to the payment processor (Stripe, for most Tithely accounts) and are unavoidable for any online giving platform. Here's what Tithely's public pricing page shows:

Credit & Debit Cards

2.9% + $0.30

Charged on every card donation. A $100 gift costs the church about $3.20 in processing.

ACH / Bank Transfer

1% + $0.30

Charged on every ACH donation. A $100 gift costs the church about $1.30 — much cheaper than cards.

Who actually pays the fees?

By default, the church absorbs the processing fee. Tithely also offers an optional "cover the fees" prompt at checkout so donors can voluntarily add the processing cost to their gift. Many churches see 60-80% of donors opt in, which effectively reduces the cost of giving close to zero for the church.

Some higher Tithely tiers may apply a small platform markup (in the range of 0.1-0.5%) on top of the base processing rate. This varies, so review your specific plan's processing terms or pull a monthly statement to see the exact effective rate.

Tithely Elevate & Church Management Pricing

Tithely Elevate is the bundled software plan. Instead of paying per product, you get the full stack — giving, church management, messaging, events, and often sites — for one monthly price. As of 2026, Tithely's public pricing lists Elevate at around $119/month for the standard tier.

What's typically in Elevate

  • Tithely Giving (online, text, and recurring)
  • Church Management System (people, groups, check-in)
  • Messaging (email and SMS)
  • Events and registrations
  • Media / sermon hosting on some tiers

How it compares to à la carte

If you'd pay for ChMS ($99/mo) plus Sites ($50+/mo) separately, Elevate is usually cheaper and includes more. If you only need online giving, stay on the free plan — there's no reason to pay $119/month just to accept donations.

Smaller churches (under 150 attendance) often don't need the full ChMS. The free giving tier plus a simple website and NFC plates on pew backs can cost under $500 one-time with zero monthly software expense.

Is Tithely Worth It for Small Churches?

For churches under 150 people, Tithely's free tier is one of the best deals in church tech. You get online giving, text giving, recurring gifts, and basic donor records for $0/month, with only the standard processing fees most donors are used to.

When the free plan is enough

  • You have a simple website already

Frequently Asked Questions About Tithely Pricing

Is Tithely really free for churches?

Yes. Tithely Giving has a free tier with no monthly fee. The church only pays payment processing fees on each transaction (2.9% + $0.30 for credit/debit, 1% + $0.30 for ACH, as publicly listed on Tithely's pricing page). Paid tiers like Tithely Elevate add software features for a monthly subscription.

What are Tithely's transaction fees in 2026?

Tithely's public pricing lists 2.9% + $0.30 per credit or debit card transaction and 1% + $0.30 per ACH bank transfer. These rates are paid to the processor on every donation. Some higher-tier plans may include small additional platform markups. Check Tithely's official pricing page for current rates.

How much does Tithely Elevate cost?

Tithely Elevate, the bundled church management suite, is publicly listed at around $119/month for the full package. Smaller ChMS-only plans can start lower (roughly $99/month), and larger multi-campus plans can run $149/month or more. Annual billing typically offers a discount. Pricing varies, so confirm with Tithely directly.

Does Tithely charge for ACH donations?

Yes, but at a lower rate than cards. Tithely's public pricing shows ACH bank transfers at 1% + $0.30 per transaction. For large gifts, ACH is meaningfully cheaper for the church than credit card giving and is worth promoting to donors.

How does Tithely Tap compare to Tap.Giving plates?

Tithely Tap sells NFC tags designed to open a Tithely giving page. Tap.Giving sells standalone NFC plates that open any giving page, including your existing Tithely page. Tap.Giving is typically less expensive per plate at volume (as low as $3.50 each for 400+) and works regardless of which Tithely plan you have, including the free tier.

Yes. Tap.Giving plates can be encoded with any URL, including your Tithely giving page. That means you keep your Tithely account, donor records, and processing rates exactly as they are, and simply add tap-to-give hardware on pew backs or chairs.
